\textbf{Term Test B version 1}

\aufgabe{5} How long will it take the world population to double at an
exponential growth rate of 1.37\% per year?

\aufgabe{5} Suppose we are preparing a lovely \emph{Canard {\`a} l'Orange} (roast duck with orange sauce). We first take our duck out of a 36$^{\circ}$F refrigerator and place it in a 350$^{\circ}$F oven to roast. After 10 minutes the internal temperature is 53$^{\circ}$F. If we want to roast the duck until just under well-done (about 170$^{\circ}$F internally), when will it be ready?

\aufgabe{5} Rewrite the expression as a single logarithm,
\begin{equation}2(\log_{5}x+2\log_{5}y-3\log_{5}z)\notag
\end{equation}

\aufgabe{5} Solve the following equation,\begin{equation}
3^{x^{2}}=175^{x-1}\notag
\end{equation}

\aufgabe{5} Solve the following equation,\begin{equation}
\ln{}2x-\ln{}4+\ln(x-2)=1\notag
\end{equation}

\aufgabe{5} Suppose that you plan to need \$10,000 in thirty-six months' time when your child starts attending university. You want to invest in an instrument yielding 3.5\% interest per year, compounded monthly. How much should you invest? Use the formula
\begin{equation}
A=P\left(1+\frac{r}{m}\right)^{mt}\notag
\end{equation}

\aufgabe{5} Rewrite so that there is no logarithm of a product, quotient, root, or power,

\end{equation}

\end{equation}
